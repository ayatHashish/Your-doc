Your Doctor Privacy Policy

What Information We Collect

We collect personal information related to healthcare from our patients and doctors through our online platform. This information may include:

  • Patient name, date of birth, contact information, insurance information, medical history, and other health-related information necessary for providing healthcare services.
  • Doctor name, contact information, medical license number, and other credentials necessary for verifying their identity and qualifications.

How We Use Your Information

We use patient and doctor information to provide healthcare services through our online platform. Specifically, we may use this information to:

  • Facilitate communication between doctors and patients regarding appointments, prescriptions, test results, and other health-related information.
  • Enable secure electronic access to patient medical records and other healthcare information.
  • Fulfill legal and regulatory requirements related to healthcare, such as record-keeping and reporting obligations.

We may also use patient information for research purposes, but only after obtaining express consent from the patient or as permitted by applicable law.

How We Disclose Your Information

We do not disclose patient or doctor information to third parties except as necessary to provide healthcare services through our platform or as required by law. Specifically, we may disclose this information to:

  • Healthcare providers, laboratories, and pharmacies involved in providing healthcare services to patients.
  • Insurance companies or other payers responsible for reimbursing healthcare services.
  • Government authorities, if required by law or to facilitate public health activities, such as disease surveillance and prevention.
  • Our service providers, who may process patient or doctor information on our behalf, but only as necessary to provide services to us and subject to appropriate confidentiality and security safeguards.

Your Rights

Patients have the right to access their personal information and request corrections of any inaccuracies. Patients may also request deletion of their personal information or withdraw consent for its use, subject to legal and regulatory requirements.

Security Measures

We take appropriate measures to protect patient and doctor information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use industry-standard security technologies and procedures, such as encryption, firewalls, and secure authentication protocols, to safeguard this information.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws and regulations. We will post the updated policy on our website and notify patients and doctors of any material changes.

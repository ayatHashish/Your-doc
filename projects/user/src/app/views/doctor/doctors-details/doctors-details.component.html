Dr. Stephanie Muh

Orthopedic Surgery - Shoulder and Elbow

25 Years of Experience

Surgeon

Dr. Stephanie Muh received her medical degree from Drexel University College of Medicine and then completed her training in Orthopaedic Surgery at Henry Ford Health System (Detroit, Michigan).

Dr Muh pursued a fellowship specialization in shoulder and elbow at Case Western Reserve University (Cleveland, Ohio).

As the Service Chief of the Shoulder and Elbow Service Line at Henry Ford Hospital, she also plays an integral role in educating medical students, residents, and fellows.

She is also the Associate Program Director for the Sports Medicine Fellowship program, as well as the Deputy Service Chief for the Department of Orthopaedics at Henry Ford-West Bloomfield Hospital.
